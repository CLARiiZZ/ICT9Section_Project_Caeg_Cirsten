THE TOP 3 MUST-VISIT PLACES IN THE PHILIPPINES

Philippines is a beautiful place filled with many, different, lovely places with a variety of food, culture, and environment. Here are 3 places that I think are must-visits when you come to Philippines.

Boracay is a resort island in the Western Visayas Region and is famous for its white sand beaches!

Bohol is a province in the Philippines in the Central Visayas Region and is well known for its chocolate hills.

Siargao is a tear-drop shaped island in the Philippine Sea and is also well known for its small reefs and white, sandy beaches.
